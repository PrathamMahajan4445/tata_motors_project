Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are the stock predictions?

Our predictions use advanced LSTM neural networks trained on 10+ years of historical data, achieving an average accuracy of 85-90% in backtesting. However, all predictions should be used for informational purposes only as market conditions can change rapidly.

What time period should I select for best results?

For optimal results, we recommend selecting at least 6 months of historical data. The model performs best with 1-3 years of data as it can better identify long-term trends and patterns.

How often is the prediction model updated?

Our models are retrained weekly with the latest market data. Major architecture updates occur quarterly to incorporate new machine learning advancements.

Can I use these predictions for actual trading?

This tool is designed for educational and research purposes only. We strongly recommend consulting with a licensed financial advisor before making any investment decisions. Past performance is not indicative of future results.
